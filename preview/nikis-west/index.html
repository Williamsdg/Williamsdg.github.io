Since 1957

A Birmingham Institution

Gus Hontzas came to the states from Greece and opened Niki's West in 1957, naming the original Niki's Restaurant after his young daughter, Nicoletta. Today, second-generation owners Pete and Teddy Hontzas carry on the family tradition.

Located right across from the Alabama Farmers Market, we serve the freshest vegetables in Birmingham alongside hearty meats, fresh seafood, and our famous homemade pies. Over 1,000 customers pass through our cafeteria line daily.
