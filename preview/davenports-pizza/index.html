About Us

A Mountain Brook Tradition

Founded in 1964 by Rex and Ardyce Hollis and named after San Francisco Giants star Jim Davenport, we've been serving our signature rectangle-cut, thin-crust pizza to Birmingham families for over 60 years.

Now in our third generation under Amanda Thames and Yates Norris, we still make our dough fresh every single day with the same tangy homemade sauce that's kept people coming back for decades.
