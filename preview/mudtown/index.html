Our Story

Named After the Mud

Creek Indians inhabited the Cahaba River banks in the early 1800s and nicknamed the area "Mudtown" due to the swampy, muddy conditions from heavy Alabama rains. We thought that was a pretty great name for a restaurant.

Since 2005, Mudtown Eat & Drink has been the neighborhood gathering spot on Green Valley Road. We serve Southern-inspired American comfort food — from our World Famous Fried Green Tomatoes to blackened ribeyes topped with blue cheese butter — alongside a full bar of craft cocktails, local beers, and curated wines.
