Our Story

The Story Behind SAW's

Mike Wilson was a North Carolina native, Johnson & Wales trained chef, and the head of Cooking Light magazine's test kitchen. He started smoking meat as a hobby — and his email list of co-workers grew to over 200 people begging for plates.

In May 2009, when the old Broadway BBQ in Homewood closed its doors, Wilson quit his day job and never looked back — and SAW's BBQ was born.

Mike passed away unexpectedly in September 2020 at age 47. His children Tuc and Reese now carry the legacy forward, keeping every recipe, every sauce, and every smoker running exactly the way their father intended.

Smoked Low & Slow

Pork shoulders, ribs, and chicken smoked for hours over real hickory and oak. Carolina-style pulled pork with a vinegar-based sauce that Mike perfected over years of backyard sessions.

Every table gets bottles of all three house sauces: tangy Carolina vinegar, sweet tomato, and Alabama white. Pick your poison or mix them all — there's no wrong answer.
